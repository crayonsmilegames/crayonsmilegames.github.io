Privacy Policy

CrayonSmile Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

CrayonSmile is a Korean sole proprietorship creating learning games for children and families. This policy explains how CrayonSmile handles privacy for apps published on Google Play.

1. Personal Information We Collect

CrayonSmile apps do not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, payment information, or advertising identifiers by default.

If a parent uses an in-app voice recording feature, the recording may be stored locally on the user's device. These files are not transmitted to CrayonSmile servers.

2. How Information Is Used

The app may use local data to provide children's learning and family play experiences. For example, a parent-recorded voice may be used inside the app for word reading or learning feedback.

3. Sharing With Third Parties

CrayonSmile does not sell or share directly collected personal information with third parties. CrayonSmile does not plan to use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or remote tracking services in its children's learning apps.

When users install or run an app through Google Play, Google may process platform operation data such as installation statistics, device information, and error reports under Google's own privacy policy and Google Play policies.

4. Retention and Deletion

Local data generated by the app is stored on the user's device. Users may delete local data through app deletion, reset features, or available in-app deletion controls.

CrayonSmile does not operate a separate server-based user account system or remote personal data database.

5. Children's Privacy

Because CrayonSmile apps are designed for children and families, CrayonSmile avoids collecting unnecessary personal information. The apps do not require children to enter personal information or transmit it externally.
