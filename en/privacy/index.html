Privacy Policy

CrayonSmile Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

CrayonSmile is a Korean sole proprietorship creating learning games for children and families. This policy explains how CrayonSmile handles privacy for apps published on Google Play, especially Somi Kids World.

1. Personal Information We Collect

CrayonSmile apps do not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, payment information, or advertising identifiers.

Somi Kids World may request microphone permission only when a parent chooses to use the family narration recording feature. Recordings are stored locally on the user's device and are not transmitted to CrayonSmile servers.

2. How Information Is Used

The app may use local data to provide children's learning and family play experiences. For example, a parent-recorded voice may be used inside the app for storybook narration or family learning experiences.

3. Sharing With Third Parties

CrayonSmile does not sell or share directly collected personal information with third parties. CrayonSmile does not use ads, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or remote tracking services in its children's learning apps.

When users install or run an app through Google Play, Google may process platform operation data such as installation statistics, device information, and error reports under Google's own privacy policy and Google Play policies.

If paid content packs are offered, payments are processed through Google Play billing. CrayonSmile does not directly collect or store payment card numbers or payment account passwords.

4. Retention and Deletion

Local data generated by the app is stored on the user's device. Users may delete local data through app deletion, reset features, or available in-app deletion controls.

CrayonSmile does not operate a separate server-based user account system or remote personal data database.

5. Children's Privacy

Because CrayonSmile apps are designed for children and families, CrayonSmile avoids collecting unnecessary personal information. The apps do not require children to enter personal information or transmit it externally. Family narration recording and content purchase management are provided through parent-focused flows.
